Features

Everything you need to
own your time

⏱️

Menu Bar Native

Lives quietly in your macOS menu bar, out of your way until you need it. See your total time tracked at a glance.

📊

Beautiful Dashboard

A stunning interactive dashboard with doughnut charts, animated progress bars, and a chronological activity timeline.

🌐

Chrome Tab Tracking

Goes beyond just apps — it tracks every Chrome tab you visit, so you know how much time was spent on YouTube vs. Gmail.

📅

Activity Timeline

A scrollable chronological timeline shows your day as a stream of colored blocks. Hover to see exact start and stop times.

🔒

100% Private

All your activity data is stored locally on your Mac in a private SQLite database. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

📤

CSV Export

Export your raw tracking data to CSV at any time to analyze it in Excel, Notion, or any other tool you prefer.

How It Works

Up and running in
under a minute

1

Download & Install

Download the .dmg file and drag ActivityTracker.app into your Applications folder. That's it — no Terminal commands required.

2

Grant Accessibility Permission

On first launch, macOS will ask you to grant Accessibility permissions so the app can see which window is currently active. This is required and your data never leaves your device.

3

Click "Start Tracking"

Click the ⏱️ icon in your menu bar and hit Start Tracking. Go about your day — ActivityTracker silently logs everything in the background.

4

View Your Dashboard

Click Daily Breakdown at any time to open a beautiful interactive dashboard in your browser showing exactly how you spent your day.
